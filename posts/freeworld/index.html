FreeWorld - Multiplayer Starter Kit

Welcome to FreeWorld, your ultimate solution for jumpstarting multiplayer projects in Unity using Pun2! FreeWorld provides a simple and clean way to integrate multiplayer functionalities into your Unity projects. Whether you’re building a game, simulation, or any other interactive experience, FreeWorld empowers you to effortlessly implement multiplayer features.

Features

  • Photon Pun2 Integration: FreeWorld seamlessly integrates with Pun2, one of the most popular Unity networking frameworks, ensuring stability and reliability for your multiplayer experiences.

  • Voice Chat: Communicate with other players in real-time using Pun Voice Chat. Whether strategizing in a game or collaborating on a project, voice communication enhances the multiplayer experience.

  • Text Chat: Exchange messages with other players through text chat functionality. Stay connected and coordinate your activities within the multiplayer world.

  • Multiplayer World: Immerse yourself in a shared multiplayer environment where you can walk around and interact with other players.
